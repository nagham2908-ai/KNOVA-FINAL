Section 01

Privacy policy.

How Knova collects, uses, and protects information about clients, prospects, and site visitors. Aligned with UAE Federal Decree-Law No. 45 of 2021 (Personal Data Protection Law).

Who we are

Knova is operated by [Entity name — to be confirmed: e.g. Knova FZ-LLC], registered in the United Arab Emirates. For privacy questions reach us at hello@knova.studio.

What we collect

Only what you give us. When you fill the contact form, we store the name, email, phone (optional), company, team size, timeline, and the description of your work you choose to share. When you email us directly, we store that thread.

The site uses no analytics that profile individuals and no third-party advertising cookies. Standard server logs (IP, user-agent, page requested) are kept for up to 30 days for security and performance, then deleted.

Why we collect it

To reply to your enquiry, to run the engagement you hire us for, and to comply with legal obligations (invoicing, tax). We do not sell, rent, or trade your data.

Who else sees it

Email goes through our mail provider. Form submissions, once the form endpoint is live, go through a transactional service (provider to be named here when chosen). These vendors are bound by their own data-protection terms. We never share your information with third parties for marketing.

Your rights

Under UAE PDPL you can ask us to show you what we hold on you, correct it, delete it, or stop using it. Email hello@knova.studio and we'll respond within 30 days. If you ever feel we mishandled your data, you can escalate to the UAE Data Office.

Retention

Active client records: for the duration of the engagement plus seven years (audit and tax). Prospect enquiries that don't convert: 24 months from last contact, then deleted. Server logs: 30 days.

Cookies

This site uses one cookie-equivalent: a localStorage flag so the intro loader is shown only once per browser. No tracking, no third-party scripts. If we add analytics later, this section will spell out what and how, and a banner will appear on first visit.
