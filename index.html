About Knova

Knova was born from two worlds — the power of technology and the passion of art.

Behind Knova stands a team of senior consultants, ERP architects and creative directors with over 10 years of hands-on experience building digital solutions for global brands. Rather than choosing between technical precision and creative excellence, we built a studio where both could exist, powered by AI to deliver faster without compromising quality.

We exist to help businesses bring their vision to life — strategically, beautifully, and digitally.
